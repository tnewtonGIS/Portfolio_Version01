About Me

I enjoy dabbling in everything geospatial with an increased focus on public health, emergency management, and tribal nations. My favorite activity involves fieldwork, collecting data, and embedding by, with and through the end users to understand agency workflows to develop a holistic system. I aim to educate agency leaders in the art of the possible by using my techniques and development designs to better serve their national and local communities. With over twenty years of federal experience, I bring those highly sought-after soft skills needed for project collaboration. Are you ready to begin?
